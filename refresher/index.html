Refresher

Pen-and-paper fluency — lycée-level prerequisites

These pages cover material the course assumes you already know. Nothing here is deep — it's all rules, patterns, and worked examples. Read the theory pages first (~30 minutes), then do the exercises to check you're fluent.

Theory

  • 1. Equation Solving Toolkit
    Isolating $x$ in every position — denominator, exponent, logarithm, square root. Exponent and log rules. Fraction arithmetic. Square root algebra. Inequalities. Systems of equations.
  • 2. Polynomials and Factoring
    What a polynomial is. The three forms of a quadratic (expanded, vertex, factored). Completing the square. Quadratic formula. Factoring patterns. Polynomial division. Rational root theorem. Binomial theorem.
  • 3. Concepts Refresher
    Complex numbers (arithmetic, modulus-argument, Euler's formula, roots of unity). Combinatorics ($n!$, $\binom{n}{k}$, stars and bars). Elementary statistics (mean, variance, standard deviation).

Exercises

10 exercises each, easy to hard, with hidden solutions. Do them on paper first.

  • 4. Equation Solving Exercises
    Linear, fractions, absolute value, square root, exponential, logarithm, systems, inequalities, trigonometric.
  • 5. Polynomial Exercises
    Quadratic formula, factoring (difference of squares, cubes, grouping), rational root theorem, polynomial division, Vieta's formulas, partial fractions.
  • 6. Concept Exercises
    Complex arithmetic, division, modulus/argument, De Moivre, combinations, binomial identities, mean/variance, hypergeometric probability.
